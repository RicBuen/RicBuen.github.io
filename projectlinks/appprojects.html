Anonymail
August 2019 - February 2020

This windows based app allows users to send private messages and/or messages that need to be privately or safely sent that cannot be done on the more mainstream email clients such as Gmail, Outlook, or Yahoo. This app does not support sending email from Anonymail to mainstream email clients such as Gmail, Outlook or Yahoo, and you can only send an recieve emails to users who have an AnonyMail account!
